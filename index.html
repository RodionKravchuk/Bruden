Our backpacks are not normal-sized. They are made in a mini size since this really is an alternative to a handbag, not solely in size but also in how you wear it.

Two shoulder straps distribute the weight more evenly across the back. This will make women feel more comfortable while walking and reduces the likelihood of muscle spasms and poor posture
